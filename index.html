01 — THE GENESIS

Redefining the Future
Since 1998

Since its inception, Techfest has grown to become Asia's Largest Science and Technology Festival, organized by the students of IIT Bombay. With a footfall exceeding 175,000 and participation from over 2,500 colleges across 78 countries, Techfest stands as a global beacon of innovation.

From robotics to artificial intelligence, from space technology to sustainable energy — Techfest provides a platform where the brightest minds converge to showcase groundbreaking ideas that shape tomorrow.
